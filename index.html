About Erika

Hi, I'm Erika.

I'm a New York and Miami-based professional with a decade of experience managing high-stakes client relationships and complex operations. I bring the same standard of care, precision, and genuine investment to every home I sit for.

I treat your home as if it were mine and your pets as if they were family. Daily photo and video updates, strict routine adherence, and round-the-clock availability — every time.

  • Background-checked, references available upon request
  • Daily photo & video updates via your preferred app
  • Strict adherence to feeding schedules & house rules
  • Plant care, mail collection & security checks included
  • Multilingual: English, Spanish, Italian & Mandarin
